GAME INSTRUCTIONS

The goal of the game is to reach the score of 500 in the alloted time.This is reached by creating rows and columns of four or three of the same emojis.

Every row/column that you find adds points.

The hint highlights two emojis that with one move can become a set of three.

SCORE COUNT

  • for a row/column of three: +15
  • for a row/column of four: +20
  • every drag: -5
  • hint: -40
